Port AI – Privacy Policy

Data storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome’s built-in storage. Nothing is sent to any server we operate.

No third-party servers

This extension does not send data to any external servers. The only optional exception: if you configure a local Ollama instance (e.g. http://localhost:11434), the extension may send conversation content to that address for summarization. Ollama runs on your machine; no data leaves your device unless you choose to run it on a remote host.

How it works

Port AI reads chat content from the page (DOM) and injects text into chat input fields. It does not collect, store remotely, or transmit your conversations outside your device.

Contact

For questions about this policy, open an issue in the extension’s source repository.
