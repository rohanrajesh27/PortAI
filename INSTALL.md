# Install Port AI in Chrome

---

## 1. Download

1. Go to [Releases](https://github.com/rohanrajesh27/PortAI/releases)
2. Download the latest `.zip` (e.g. `PortAI-v0.2.0.zip`)

---

## 2. Extract

1. Find the zip in your Downloads folder
2. Double-click to extract
3. Keep the resulting folder (e.g. `PortAI-v0.2.0`)

---

## 3. Open Chrome Extensions

1. Open Chrome
2. Go to `chrome://extensions`
3. Turn **Developer mode** ON (top-right)

---

## 4. Load the extension

1. Click **Load unpacked**
2. Select the folder you extracted
3. Confirm it contains `manifest.json`

---

## 5. Confirm

The Port AI icon appears in your toolbar (you may need to click the puzzle piece and pin it).

---
